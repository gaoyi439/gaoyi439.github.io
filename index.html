Biography

My research interests mainly include machine learning and weakly supervised learning. Currently, I am an assistant researcher (至善博后) at the PALM Group , School of Computer Science and Engineering, Southeast University, China. I received the Ph.D. degree from Southeast University, China in 2025, advised by Prof. Min-Ling Zhang and the B.Sc., M.Sc. degrees from Northwest University, China, in 2017 and 2020 respectively.
